A new Look, a new Style

Replacing Flatlock

With three distinct stitches (INFUSED, COMFORT and SLIM) to choose from, Activeseam™ is designed to replace Flatlock. Activeseam™ is a more comfortable seam, a more technical stitch type, and a more aesthetically interesting stitch!

A new Market

Branding

In the 1920s Lacoste™ added a crocodile to a shirt and instantly created branded apparel. In the 1970s Gortex® branded material. Seams and stitching are the last element of apparel that are delivered to consumers without specific value. Merrow has changed this with ACTIVESEAM™!

Infused

The signature version of Activeseam™is the Infused stitch. A three thread stitch that allows a designer to add depth and color to the seam. The Infused stitch may be sewn open or closed, and provides strength advantages in addition to visual distinction.

Comfort

A two thread seam, the Comfort stitch is designed to be soft on the skin. A Comfort seam may be used on everything from Yoga clothing delicate lingerie.

Slim

The two thread Slim Activeseam™ presents both a new design option as well as a comfortable alternative to simple overlock seaming. For apparel that require discrete detail, the Slim Activeseam™ is the perfect choice.

ActiveSeam™ Features

Innovative Flat Overlock Stitching

Technical

Designed by Merrow® to satisfy strict comfort and aesthetic requirements, ActiveSeam™ minimizes the number of threads used in the stitch to maintain a flat profile. Material is butted within the stitch - not lapped or bunched - ensuring a low profile and comfortable next to skin experience.

Stretchable

ActiveSeam™ has nearly 100% linear travel and will stretch with fabric, meaning movement is free and unrestricted. Garments with stretch properties are no longer inhibited by their stitching.

Versatile

ActiveSeam™ is available as three different configurations to suit your material and aesthetic. Use ActiveSeam Slim™ on thin, lightweight material or Infused™ when you really want ActiveSeam™ to stand out. An of course, ActiveSeam Comfort™ is where it all started and covers a wide range of materials and aesthetics.
