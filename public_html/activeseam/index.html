Patchlight Features

THE MOST VERSATILE HIGH PERFORMANCE LIGHT FOR ATHLETICS and SAFETY

Light

Weighing 5 Grams, the Patchlight can be carried anywhere easily. Patchlight was designed with featherweight materials, and a low profile to fit anywhere and be worn or carried by the ultra-light backpackers and cyclists.

Bright

80 Lumens, visible up to a 1/2 Mile. This is one very bright light. The Patchlight has four Modes: Fast Strobe, Slow Strobe, Steady On and Off

Beautiful

We worked with the product design team at Merrow to develop a beautiful product - Patchlight looks great on your pack, road bike or stroller. It just fits. We know how important our gear is to us, and Patchlight was built to make it look even better.
