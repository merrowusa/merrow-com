Merrow Optical Auto-Emblem

Technical Specifications

1 Operator Skill Level Required: Unskilled.
2 Sewing Head: MG3U AE Type. Note: The MG3U Automatic Edging Machine requires a unique variation of the MG3U. A standard MG3U cannot be retrofitted for this purpose, and we will not accept orders for the MG3U AEM for use with a used or retrofitted MG3U sewing head.
3 Hopper Capacity: 700 emblems maximum, depending on material thickness.
4 Emblem Size: 1" x 1" to 5" x 5" (25.4mm x 25.4mm to 127mm x 127mm). Modifications can be made to accommodate smaller or larger sizes.
5 Voltage: 120 V AC.
6 Air Pressure: 60 PSI. Average demand: 40 PSI.
7 Dimensions: 28" L x 32" W x 60" H including thread stand (711.2mm L x 812.8mm W x 1524mm H)
8 Weight: 150 lbs (68 K.)
9 Radius Minimum on Emblems: 3/8" (9.652mm)
10 Maximum Size of Rectangular Emblems: 4" x 6" (101.6mm x 152.4mm)
11 Maximum Recommended Speed: 4000 spm.
12 Stitch Length: 24-36 per inch (24-36 per 25.4mm)
