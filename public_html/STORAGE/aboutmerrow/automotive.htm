Automotive

Merrow helps the automotive industry keep down costs by enabling more-continuous fabric processing for essential materials like headliners and cloth interiors made from a foam and fabric laminate.

Merrow flat butt-seam stitching is an essential part of the process for the production of rubber fan belts, hoses, cog-drive belts, serpentine drive belts, and more.
