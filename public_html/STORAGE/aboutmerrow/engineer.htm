About Merrow: Advanced Engineering

Merrow has exclusive engineering, design and metallurgy expertise. We’re the only manufacturer in the world building cam-driven sewing machines that stand up to heavy-duty use. From blueprint to finished product, we make everything we put into our machines. That’s the difference between a manufacturer and an assembly shop. Quality, precision and constant parts availability are the advantages Merrow delivers to your operation.

Our cam-driven machines produce a superior quality stitch. Using sophisticated 5-axis computerized milling machines, we’ve succeeded in developing proprietary high-technology components offering faster, quieter, operation and solid durability.

From the finest silk to the toughest leather, even fiberglass, Merrow has a precision feeding and stitching solution for your most demanding material processing requirements. We’ll customize our machines to meet your current challenges or pursue entirely new applications.

We designed our screws and precision thread standard that’s consistent across all our lines, going back to before 1900. We use only the highest quality materials in our components for extra strength and consistent performance.
