Textile Processing

Merrow is an acknowledged leader in assembly line automation and specializes in joining and splicing materials for continuous processing. Companies that implement continuous processing typically receive a very high return on investment by:

  • Eliminating waste
  • Reducing damage to processing equipment
  • Minimizing downtime
  • Speeding up production

In addition to developing stitching for continuous processing of common textile fabrics like cotton, fleece, denim, wool, silk, felt, etc., Merrow has expertise with a variety of backing and laminate materials – like the proprietary membrane used in the production of windproof fleece – as well as non-apparel applications using both woven and non-woven materials and fabrics. Our machines are uniquely suited to the demands of industrial use due to their proven reliability and long-lasting performance.

If you would like to explore how prodution line automation or continuous processing can help you achieve economies of scale and contribute to your bottom line, contact Merrow at: textileprocessing@merrow.com
