Wire-Reinforced Fabrics

When you need to bring dimensional characteristics to fabric, Merrow has the answers. Merrow machines are used to sew wire structural elements inside pollution filter material to create replaceable environmental filters that protect air quality and contribute to a healthier workplace. The same technique is used to sew wire within ribbon to produce special decorative ribbon used by florists and craftspeople.
