Netting Material

Merrow can also sew netting directly to rope reinforcement material or stitch rope or cord within tape or mesh material of various widths and compositions. The most common applications for this capability are for saftey nets used for bridge construction, high iron work, amusement park attractions, volleyball and badminton nets, bait fish nets, and hang bags used for meat packing and smoke-curing wild-game meats.
