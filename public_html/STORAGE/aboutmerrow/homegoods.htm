Home Goods and Apparel

Merrow has a long history of innovation in home goods and apparel production. The company pioneered overedge stitching and is a leader in the textile industry. Its machines are used to seam and finish pillow cases, slipcovers, tablecloths, towels, washcloths and blankets. Apparel applications include socks/hosiery, decorative seaming and toe-closing.
