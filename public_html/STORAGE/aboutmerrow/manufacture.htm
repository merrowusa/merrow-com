Manufacturing

Leading farm and heavy equipment manufacturers rely on a well-known tire and rubber company for sturdy drive and fan belts. The producer turned to Merrow to develop a solution essential to its manufacturing process. The assignment: to develop a high-strength, no-overlap stitch for the rubber belts’ cloth backing. Merrow developed a proprietary butted seam stitch which secured the fabric on a cylindrical mandrel during a high-stress manufacturing process.
