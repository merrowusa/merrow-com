About Merrow: Our Solutions

Solutions Are What We’re All About.

When you work with Merrow, the starting point is the end result you want to achieve. We specialize in solving unique production and engineering challenges to give you a cost and quality advantage. A case in point is our sample stitch program. Depending on the required application, we may have a machine that will meet your needs as is. Or, we will build a machine to order, or modify an existing machine, to satisfy your unique requirements. Unparalleled partnership and responsive service is why Merrow is the first choice whenever you need to configure a special production line or improve an existing process.
