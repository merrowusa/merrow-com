Merrow Solutions

  
 

Apparel Manufacturing

A principal producer and retailer of women’s intimate apparel and swimwear sought Merrow’s assistance in the production of a shawl collar on a popular bathrobe style. The retailer wanted a quality, uniform stitch that could be stitched on a single pass through the sewing machine. Merrow adapted an existing machine with a special mechanism to double productivity, reduce cost, and improve the quality of the finished garment.
