Synthetic Non-Woven Materials

When conditions are too tough for welded seams, Merrow stitching can stand up to the heat and stress. Synthetic material used for re-lining water-pipes, acrylic polymer and acetate films are all materials that Merrow has successfully joined roll-to-roll without bumps or lumps. Other applications include manufacturing road signs and license plates that are printed on a reflective material that must be held in place under high heat and heavy pressure during production.
