Fire and Safety

Firefighters, police, road crews, airport personnel and other public safety workers rely on reflective tape and other light management materials for safety on the job. Merrow helps the leading manufacturers of reflective products used around the world to process embossed, printed, laminated or bonded optical microstructures, which are used on clothing, signs, structures and vehicles. This life-saving technology makes our roads safer and protects those who look after our safety, every day.
