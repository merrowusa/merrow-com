Increase your production and quality by automating functions on many applications. Servo motors control speed and stop / start functions and are programmable to stop the machine with the needle in the up position or stop with the needle in the down position. These features allow servo motors to serve as platforms for other devices.

Pneumatic Foot Lifter: Works with servo motor. Heeling the treadle lifts the presser foot automatically.

Thread Tension Release: Works with pneumatic foot lifter for quick release of goods.

Automatic Edge Sensor: An electric eye detects the material. Ideal for automating operations like overedging of napkins, tablecloths and straight line operations. With one push of the treadle, the machine will sew at the programmed speed until the edge sensor stops the machine when it detects the corner or end of the material.
